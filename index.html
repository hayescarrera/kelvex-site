Energy optimization

Stop paying for demand you don't understand.

Demand charges can represent 40% of your utility bill — and most operators have no idea which equipment is setting their peak or when it's happening. Kelvex shows you exactly where the money is going and automates the controls that reduce it.

  • Real-time demand tracked against your tariff structure
  • Automated pre-cooling before peak rate windows
  • Load controls triggered on demand thresholds
  • Monthly cost trending across your entire portfolio
Compliance

Audit-ready logs, automatically.

FDA, USDA, and HACCP temperature records don't need to be a manual process. Kelvex logs everything continuously and generates formatted reports on demand — so your team isn't spending hours pulling data when an inspector shows up.

  • Continuous temperature logs with no gaps or manual entries
  • One-click report generation for any date range and zone
  • Full excursion history with timestamps and cause data
  • Configurable alarm thresholds per zone and zone type
