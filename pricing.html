Common questions

What counts as a facility?

A facility is a single physical location — a warehouse, distribution center, or production site. Multiple buildings on the same property under one management team typically count as one facility. We'll confirm this with you before you commit to anything.

Do I need to replace my existing equipment?

No. Kelvex connects to your existing compressor controllers and sensors. If you have equipment we haven't integrated yet, we'll add it — that's been true for every customer we've onboarded so far.

How long does it take to go live?

Most customers are seeing live data within the same week of signing. We handle the connection setup and configuration — your team's involvement is minimal, and we don't require any contractors or new wiring.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

We offer month-to-month and annual options. Annual billing comes with a meaningful discount. We don't lock you into multi-year terms, but in practice customers tend to stay once they've seen what they were missing.

What does onboarding look like?

We walk through your facilities with you, configure your controller connections, set your alert thresholds, and make sure your team knows how to use the dashboard. We don't hand you a PDF and disappear.

Can I start with one facility and expand later?

Yes — and most customers do. You pay per facility, so adding a second or third site is straightforward. Portfolio pricing kicks in at six or more and is applied retroactively if you grow into it.

Not sure which tier is right?

Tell us about your operation and we'll point you in the right direction. Most customers know within the first conversation.
